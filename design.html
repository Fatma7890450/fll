Bio-Inspired Scorpion Robot

A smart ground robot inspired by scorpion biology, designed to safely inspect and excavate archaeological sites while preserving fragile cultural heritage.

Explore Design Project Value

Site Selection & Engineering Design

Excavation Site Selection

The excavation site is selected with extreme care to avoid damaging archaeological remains. Environmental sensitivity, soil conditions, and surrounding artifacts are all considered to ensure safe operation.

Engineering Design Approach

The robot’s engineering design is tailored to the excavation environment. Sensor placement is optimized for accuracy, efficiency, and minimal interference with fragile archaeological elements.

Bio-Inspired Scorpion Concept

Biological Inspiration

Inspired by scorpion biology, the robot achieves an effective balance between stability and mobility, enabling it to operate efficiently in complex archaeological environments.

Compact Ground Robot

Its small size allows movement within narrow and confined spaces, making it ideal for archaeological inspections where human access is limited or risky.

Mechanical & Kinematic Characteristics

Multiple Legs

The multi-legged structure provides high stability while moving on uneven or fragile surfaces, which is essential for protecting archaeological sites.

High Mobility & Flexibility

The scorpion-inspired form allows smooth maneuvering in tight spaces without disturbing surrounding archaeological elements.

Movable Tail

A movable tail is used for precise excavation and inspection tasks, ensuring minimal disturbance to the archaeological environment.

Sensors & Safety Systems

Obstacle Avoidance

Advanced sensors detect obstacles in real time, allowing the robot to navigate safely without damaging artifacts or structures.

Soil Analysis

Soil analysis sensors help assess ground conditions, supporting safe and informed excavation decisions.

Scientific & Practical Value

Safety

Reduces human risk during archaeological exploration in hazardous or hard-to-reach areas.

Precision

Enables accurate inspection and excavation while preserving fragile archaeological remains.

Sustainability

Contributes to the protection and long-term sustainability of human cultural heritage.
