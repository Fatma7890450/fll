Techno Relics

An innovative autonomous ground robot designed to revolutionize archaeological excavation through intelligent automation, enhanced safety, and high-precision documentation.

Project Overview

Techno Relics is a smart autonomous ground robot capable of navigating rough and complex terrains while automatically correcting its position when losing balance or tipping over. It supports semi-autonomous excavation operations by identifying sensitive archaeological layers before any human intervention, ensuring site preservation.

The robot documents archaeological sites and tombs with high accuracy using 3D imaging technologies, creating a complete digital model of the site. It also analyzes inscriptions and artifacts to identify the related civilization and historical period, and processes ancient maps to transmit precise site locations.

In addition, Techno Relics enhances site protection by monitoring excavation activities and intelligently handling hazardous terrains to reduce human risk. It collects multi-source data, including soil electrical conductivity, visual imagery, and AI-based analysis, delivering comprehensive and accurate reports on potential archaeological locations.

Key Features

High Autonomy

Self-navigation, balance correction, and intelligent decision-making with minimal human input.

3D Documentation

High-resolution 3D scanning and full digital reconstruction of archaeological sites.

Intelligent Analysis

AI-driven analysis of artifacts and inscriptions to determine civilization and era.

Site Protection

Continuous monitoring and safe interaction with dangerous terrains to protect both people and sites.

Comprehensive Reports

Integrated sensor data and AI insights delivered in fast, accurate, and actionable reports.
